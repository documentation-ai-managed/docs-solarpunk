---
title: Cannot Access Workspace
description: How to resolve workspace access issues.
---

## Symptoms

- "Workspace not found" error
- Access denied on project page

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check login status">
    ````
    Make sure you are logged in to your Solarpunk account.
    If not, log in again via https://app.solarpunk.com/login
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify workspace membership">
    ````
    Ask your admin to confirm your invitation was sent and accepted.
    Try re-accepting the workspace invite email.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact support">
    ````
    If still stuck, email support@solarpunk.com with your workspace and user email.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
