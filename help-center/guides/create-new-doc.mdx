---
title: Creating a Documentation Page
description: How to add a new doc page in Solarpunk.
---

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your project">
    ````
    Go to https://app.solarpunk.com
    Click your project to open it.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a page">
    ````
    Click the "+ New Page" button in the sidebar.
    Give your page a name.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write and save">
    ````
    Add content using Markdown or the editor.
    Click "Save" to publish changes.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
