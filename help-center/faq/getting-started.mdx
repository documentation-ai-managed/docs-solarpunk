## Getting Started

To create your first Solarpunk project:

1. Log in at `https://app.solarpunk.com`
2. Click the **New Project** button on the dashboard
3. Enter a name and optional description
4. Click **Create**

Your project is ready. Use the sidebar to add documentation pages or invite collaborators.

Need a workspace? Reach out to your admin or contact support.
